Our story

Built by a woman who believed Chennai needed a gentler way to learn to drive

Fifteen-plus years later, that idea is a 4.9★ driving school trusted by 778 reviewers — gents and ladies alike.

Why we exist

Learning to drive shouldn't feel like a test you're failing in public

Most learners don't struggle with the mechanics of driving — they struggle with the fear of being watched, honked at, or judged while they're still learning. Radha Driving School was built around removing that fear first, and teaching the driving second. As a women-owned institution in Adyar, we set out to make sure every gent and every lady who walked in nervous, walked out capable of handling real Chennai traffic on their own.

Reviewers consistently describe the same thing in different words: instructors who correct without scolding, who stay beside you until the nervousness is gone, and who treat "confidence building" as the actual product — not just the licence at the end of it.
