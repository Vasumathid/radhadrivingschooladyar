Courses

Six ways to learn, one standard of patience

Every course below is taught by instructors our learners name by name in their reviews. Message us for current batch timings and fees.

Most requested

Four‑Wheeler Driving

Manual and hatchback (including Levo 80) lessons that take you from clutch control and parking to confident city driving in real Chennai traffic.

  • Basics: seating, mirrors, clutch/gear feel, controlled starts
  • Practice on actual roads and signals, not just an empty ground
  • RTO test preparation and licence assistance included

Two‑Wheeler Driving

Bike and scooter training for absolute beginners and for riders who already balance a two-wheeler but freeze in heavy traffic.

  • Balance and control on quiet roads first
  • Guided rides through real traffic areas, led by an instructor
  • Ladies-only batches available on request

Gearless Three‑Wheeler

Focused training for gearless auto driving — a specialised course that few driving schools in Chennai offer.

  • Auto-specific handling, turning and reversing practice
  • Traffic navigation built for three-wheeler width and turning radius
  • Licence category guidance included
Woman-led

Ladies‑Special Batches

Car and two-wheeler batches taught by women instructors, for learners who want a space with zero hesitation and zero judgement.

  • Instructed by our women trainers, at a pace you set
  • Flexible timings built around home and work schedules
  • Same real-traffic practice as every other course

Simulation Class

Simulator sessions that build reflexes and muscle memory before a learner ever sits behind a real wheel — especially useful for first-timers.

  • Low-pressure environment to learn control basics
  • Builds confidence before first on-road lesson
  • Recommended for anxious or completely new learners

RTO Licence Assistance

Every course includes guidance through the RTO process — our team has accompanied learners to the RTO for over 15 years.

  • Documentation and application guidance
  • Test-day accompaniment
  • Support until the licence is in your hand
